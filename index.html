Scenic Highway 140 Closed Indefinitely

A rockslide has brought several tons of boulders down onto Highway 140, a major route leading into Yosemite National Park. Due to government and administration technicalities, Yosemite employees are unable to provide a re-open date at this time and thus have declared Highway 140 closed indefinitely for the time being.
